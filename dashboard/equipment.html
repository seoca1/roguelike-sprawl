🔨 Crafting System (3-tier)

제작 흐름: 5 raw → 4 components → final equipment

[Tier 1] RAW MATERIALS (5 types)
  ├─ scrap_metal
  ├─ data_fragment
  ├─ ice_shard
  ├─ biotech_tissue
  └─ nano_dust
        ↓
[Tier 2] COMPONENTS (4 types)
  ├─ circuit_board (3× scrap_metal + 1× data_fragment)
  ├─ processor_chip (2× ice_shard + 1× data_fragment)
  ├─ biotech_membrane (2× biotech_tissue + 1× nano_dust)
  └─ neural_interface (2× data_fragment + 1× biotech_tissue)
        ↓
[Tier 3] FINAL EQUIPMENT (per slot)
  ├─ DECK: 1× processor_chip + 1× circuit_board
  ├─ EYEWARE: 1× processor_chip + 1× neural_interface
  ├─ BODYSUIT: 2× biotech_membrane + 1× circuit_board
  └─ ... (per body slot)
