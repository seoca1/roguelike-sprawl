🎭 Cast & Archetypes

9 cast characters (story) × 4 archetypes (gameplay) — the game's character systems

The game has two separate character systems:

Cast (9) — Story characters. Each has a chapter. case / sil / kas / suit / 3jane / angie / sally / wigan / neuromancer

Archetypes (4) — Gameplay POV. Mission archetype. novice (15) / veteran (15) / heretic (12) / suit (5) — 47 total

The 9 cast names appear in chapters.json and arcs.json character field.
The 4 archetypes appear in missions.json story.character_ref field.

Each archetype maps 1:1 to a primary cast: novice→case, veteran→sil, heretic→kas, suit→suit.
The remaining 5 cast (3jane, angie, sally, wigan, neuromancer) are cutscene / chapter-only — no direct mission POV.
