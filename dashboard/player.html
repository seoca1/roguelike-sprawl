ICE HP Scaling Formula

Grade > Tier: HP = hp_base + (hp_per_grade × grade_diff)
Grade < Tier: HP = hp_base × max(0.7, 1.0 + grade_diff × 0.15)
Example: Watchdog (hp_base=50, hp_per_grade=10, tier=1)
Grade 1 → HP = 50 + (10 × 0) = 50
Grade 3 → HP = 50 + (10 × 2) = 70
Grade 5 → HP = 50 + (10 × 4) = 90
Grade 1 vs Black (tier=3) → HP = 200 × 0.7 = 140 (minimum 70%)
