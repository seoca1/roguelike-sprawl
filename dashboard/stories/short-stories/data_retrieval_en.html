Data Retrieval

도입

*"The sky above the port was the color of television, tuned to a dead channel."*

— Gibson, Neuromancer, Chapter 1

전개

You jack in at the Sprawl Hole, the familiar cold of the trodes spreading across your temples. The matrix unfolds around you, a maze of data streams and corporate architecture. The target is a Hosaka subsidiary—not the big leagues, but big enough to have something worth hiding.

"Packet's ready," The Finn says. "Corporate firewall, mid-level security. You should be in and out before they notice."

"Should be," you say.

"Don't get greedy."

The entry node is a simple router, the kind you see a thousand times in any given run. You move through it quickly, following the data streams toward the target. The server architecture is standard Hosaka—clean lines, efficient security, nothing fancy. The kind of place that thinks it's untouchable.

You find the data node in the corporate depths. It's buried under three layers of encryption, but your deck makes quick work of that. You reach out with your probe, feeling the connection form.

The data transfers.

But something's wrong.

절정

The file is bigger than it should be. Instead of the small data packet you expected, this is a full database—employee records, financial reports, something that looks like payroll information. Corporate secrets, the kind that could get people fired. Or worse.

"Finn," you say. "This isn't the right file."

Silence on the other end. Then: "Check the metadata."

You do. The file is tagged with a corporate code you recognize—Tessier-Ashpool. A subsidiary you've never heard of, but the name is everywhere in the Sprawl. The megacorp behind half the data streams you're surfing through.

"This is T-A," you say. "What's T-A doing with Hosaka's payroll?"

"What do you care?" The Finn's voice is different now. Harder. "You got paid. Take the job and run."

The data streams around you are starting to shift. You can feel the corporate security waking up, sensing the intrusion. You've been in here too long.

"Jack out," The Finn says. "Now."

You do.

결말

The Sprawl Hole materializes around you. The Finn is watching you with that expression he always has, like he's calculating how much you're worth. The data is already gone, transferred to a location you don't know.

"What was that?" you ask.

"What do you mean?"

"T-A. You knew what was in that file."

The Finn doesn't answer. He just pours himself another drink and looks away. After a while, you realize he's not going to.

"Get some rest," he says finally. "Tomorrow's going to be a long day."

You go.

But the data stays with you, burning in your memory like a brand. Corporate secrets. Payroll information. A thread that connects Hosaka to Tessier-Ashpool in ways you don't understand yet.

The Sprawl is full of threads like that. Pull on one, and you never know what you'll find.

You pull on your blanket and try to sleep.

The data follows you into your dreams.
