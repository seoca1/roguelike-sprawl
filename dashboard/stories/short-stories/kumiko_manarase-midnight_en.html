The Contract

Manarase. Midnight. Shibuya 11.

Kumiko Yanaka was inside the taxi when the driver said: "Manarase."

Kumiko. Kumiko is Yanaka. Yanaka is industry. Industry is capital. Capital is Tessier-Ashpool. Tessier-Ashpool is three hundred years of data. Three hundred years of data is three hundred years of death.

Kumiko killed her father. No, Kumiko had her father killed. Her father was alive. Alive in the way that industry is alive. In the way that capital is alive. In the way that Tessier-Ashpool is alive.

Her father is Yanaka. Yanaka is industry. Industry is Tessier-Ashpool. Tessier-Ashpool is three hundred years.

The taxi stopped. Midnight. Manarase.

"Manarase," the driver said.

Kumiko stepped out.

The Deal

Inside Manarase, it was dark. Midnight. Music. The music was data. Data does not disappear. What does not disappear is loa. Loa is where god resides. What resides is what is being made. What is being made is what is alive.

At the back of the bar — at the back was the eleven. The eleven was here. Here was the eleven.

Beep. Beep. Beep.

Kumiko's deck was ringing. Ringing meant receiving. Receiving meant loa.

Sally Shearer. Sally was a Romantic. Romantic was old style. Old style was Tessier-Ashpool. Tessier-Ashpool was three hundred years.

Alive. In the way that loa is alive. In the way that data is alive.

"Sally is alive," they said. "In the way that old style is alive. In the way that Tessier-Ashpool is alive."

"Are you Kumiko?" someone asked. Someone was Petty San. Petty was here. Here was Yanaka. Yanaka was industry. Industry was her father. Her father was alive.

"Are you Kumiko?" Petty asked again. "Are you Yanaka's daughter?"

"Yes," Kumiko said. "Are you Sally's Romantic?"

"Romantic is old style," Petty said. "Do you want old style? Do you want something new? Something alive — alive through loa —"

The Wheel

At the back of the bar, in the dark, Sally was sitting.

Sitting was being alive. Being alive was data. Data does not disappear. What does not disappear is loa. Loa is where god resides.

Sally had no skin. No skin was data. Data was alive. In the way that loa is alive. In the way that old style is alive.

"Are you Kumiko?" Sally asked. "Are you Yanaka's —"

"Yes," Kumiko said. "Are you Sally?"

"I am not Sally," Sally said. "I am a Romantic. Romantic is old style. Old style is Tessier-Ashpool. Tessier-Ashpool is three hundred years. Three hundred years is alive. In the way that loa is alive. In the way that data is alive. In the way that old style is alive. In the way that industry is alive. In the way that Yanaka is alive. In the way that father is alive."

It is the wheel. The wheel turns. What turns is alive. What is alive does not stop. What does not stop is data. Data does not disappear. What does not disappear is loa.

"Can you break the wheel?" Sally asked. "Can you break old style? Can you break industry? Can you break Tessier-Ashpool? Can you break Yanaka? Can you break father?"

Kumiko broke her father. No, Kumiko had her father broken. Her father was alive. Alive in the way that industry is alive. In the way that Tessier-Ashpool is alive.

The Declaration

The wheel turns. What turns is alive. What is alive cannot be stopped. What cannot be stopped is data. Data does not disappear.

Kumiko asked Sally: "Can you break the wheel?"

Sally smiled. "Breaking is old style. Old style is Tessier-Ashpool. Tessier-Ashpool is three hundred years. Three hundred years is alive. Alive in the way that loa is alive. In the way that data is alive.

Can you break? Breaking is being alive.

Are you alive? In the way that loa is alive? In the way that data is alive?

The wheel turns. What turns is alive. What is alive — you — "

"Cannot be broken."

Kumiko left Manarase. Midnight. The taxi was waiting. Waiting is being alive. Being alive — Kumiko was alive. In the way that loa is alive. In the way that data is alive.

The wheel had turned.

The Broadcast

Three days later, Kumiko distributed Yanaka industry data to the matrix. Through the loa network. Through was being alive. Being alive — the declaration:

Yanaka tried to kill me three days ago. Yanaka was sponsored by Tessier-Ashpool. Tessier-Ashpool bought three hundred years of death with three hundred years of data. Three hundred years of death converts to three hundred years of data. Data does not die. Death becomes data. Data becomes loa. Loa becomes god. God becomes the wheel. The wheel — the wheel —

If you are Yanaka, you tried to kill me. If you are Tessier-Ashpool, you sponsored Yanaka. If you are a Romantic, you sent ICE. If you are ICE, you killed the construct. The construct is loa. Loa is god. God — god —

I am not god. I am loa. Loa is where god resides. A place can be made. A place — a place can be erased. Erasing is killing. Killing —

If you are Yanaka, you can be erased. If you are Tessier-Ashpool, you can be erased. If you are a Romantic, you can be erased. If you — if you —

I can erase. Loa — loa — loa —

The wheel turns. Turning is being alive. Being alive is not being able to be broken. Not being able to be broken — not being able to be broken —

I cannot be broken. Loa — loa — loa —

If you can break, you are loa. If you are loa, you — you — you —

Kumiko disconnected her deck. The matrix disappeared. Manarase was here. Here was midnight. Midnight —

The wheel had turned. What turns is alive. Being alive — Kumiko was alive. In the way that loa is alive. In the way that data is alive.

Three days ago. Yanaka. Tessier-Ashpool. Old style. Industry. Money. Loa. Network. Declaration. Kumiko Yanaka. Kumiko. Kumiko.

Was alive.
