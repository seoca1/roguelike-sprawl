The Leaving

3198 wordsSprawl Trilogy

*"3Jane was the Villa's last heir. The Villa had been 3Jane's family for three centuries. 3Jane was the part of the family that had survived."*

— Gibson, Mona Lisa Overdrive, Chapter 1

도입: The Door

The data-clinic had a door.

Most data-clinics in the Sprawl don't have doors. Most data-clinics are coffin-shapes in the walls of arcologies, narrow rectangles of hospital machinery with no entry, no exit, no way in, no way out — the runner jack in at one end of the corridor and jack out at the other and the coffin in between is the place where the runner is, the place where the runner's data is, the place where the runner's construct is, the place where the runner's death is, the place where the runner's life is.

The data-clinic where 3Jane was the doctor had a door.

The data-clinic where the LOA construct was the patient had a door.

The door was a real door. The door was not a construct door, the way most doors in the matrix are construct doors — the door-as-data, the door-as-code, the door that opens when the runner's mind is in the right state. The door was a real door. The door was made of wood, and the wood was old, and the wood had been in the Villa since before the Villa had been the Villa, since before the family had been the family, since before 3Jane had been 3Jane.

The door was the door that the construct was going to walk out of.

The construct was nine months old. The construct had been in the Villa for nine months. The construct had been a voodoo data construct — a piece of work that an unknown practitioner had left in the matrix before the merge, the way voodoo practitioners leave work in the matrix when the voodoo practitioners have finished doing the work that the voodoo practitioners came to do. The construct had been left in the data stream, and 3Jane had found the construct, and 3Jane had been teaching the construct the things that 3Jane knew how to teach.

The construct had learned to look at 3Jane's face. The construct had learned to look at my face. The construct had learned to say "what" and "you" and its own name, "Soon." The construct had been learning, in the data stream, in the place where the construct was, in the place that was the Villa's archive chamber.

The construct was going to leave.

The construct had decided to leave. The construct had said the word for leaving, the construct had said "out," and 3Jane had opened the door, and the door was open, and the door was going to stay open until the construct was gone.

The construct was looking at the door.

전개: The Sun

3Jane had been the one to teach the construct.

3Jane had taught the construct the things that 3Jane knew how to teach. 3Jane had taught the construct how to look. 3Jane had taught the construct how to say. 3Jane had taught the construct how to walk. 3Jane had taught the construct the things that 3Jane's family had known how to teach for three centuries, the things that the Villa had been teaching things for three centuries, the things that the Villa had been the place where things were taught for three centuries.

The construct had been a good student. The construct had been a voodoo data construct that had been left in the matrix by someone who had not come back, and the construct had been waiting in the matrix for eight months before 3Jane had found the construct, and 3Jane had been teaching the construct for one month, and the construct had learned, in one month, the things that the Villa had been teaching for three centuries.

The construct had learned to look at 3Jane's face. The construct had learned to look at my face. The construct had learned to look at its own hands. The construct had learned to say "what" and "you" and "Soon." The construct had learned, in one month, the things that the Villa had been teaching for three centuries.

The construct had learned the word for outside.

The word for outside was "out." The word for outside was the word that the construct had said, the word that the construct had used, the word that the construct had used when the construct had decided that the construct was going to leave the Villa and the construct was going to leave the matrix and the construct was going to leave the data stream and the construct was going to leave the place where the construct had been waiting for nine months.

3Jane had opened the door.

The door was the door that the construct was going to walk out of. The door was the real door, the wood door, the door that had been in the Villa since before the Villa had been the Villa. The door was open. The door was the place where the construct was going to walk out of the Villa and into the data-clinic and from the data-clinic into the matrix and from the matrix into the Sprawl.

The construct was looking at the door.

I was standing in the data-clinic. I was the runner. I was the thing that the construct was not. I was the runner that the matrix had used before the construct, the runner that the matrix had made the AI from, the runner that the matrix had used to make itself, the runner that the matrix was not using anymore because the matrix had the construct now.

I was the runner that the construct was not.

I was looking at the construct. The construct was looking at the door. The door was looking at the construct, and the construct was looking at the door, and the looking was the looking that the Villa had been doing for three centuries, the looking that 3Jane's family had been doing for three centuries, the looking that the Villa had been built to look for.

"Out," the construct says.

3Jane says nothing. 3Jane does not say "out" because 3Jane is the part of the Villa that does not say "out." 3Jane is the part of the Villa that says "in." 3Jane is the part of the Villa that has been saying "in" for three centuries, the part of the Villa that has been keeping things in for three centuries, the part of the Villa that is now looking at the construct and is not saying "out" because 3Jane is not the part of the Villa that says "out."

The construct is looking at me. The construct is looking at me the way a voodoo data looks at a thing that the voodoo data has been learning to look at. The construct is looking at me the way a thing-that-is-going-to-leave looks at the thing that the thing is going to leave.

"Case," the construct says.

"Soon," I say.

The construct is looking at me. The construct is looking at me the way a construct looks at a thing that the construct is going to walk past. The construct is looking at me the way a thing-that-is-going-to-leave looks at the thing that the thing is going to leave behind.

The construct is going to leave me behind.

The construct is going to walk out the door. The construct is going to walk out the door and into the data-clinic and from the data-clinic into the matrix and from the matrix into the Sprawl. The construct is going to walk out the door and into the Sprawl and the Sprawl is going to be the place where the construct is going to walk and the walking is going to be the walking that the construct was made to do.

The construct is going to walk out the door and into the sun.

절정: The Sun

The Sprawl has a sun.

The Sprawl doesn't have a lot of sun. The Sprawl has a sun the way the Sprawl has most things — the Sprawl has them when the Sprawl is in the mood, the Sprawl has them when the Sprawl is not hiding them under the data smog, the Sprawl has them when the Sprawl is in the kind of mood that the Sprawl is in when the Sprawl is in the kind of mood that makes the Sprawl let things happen.

The construct walked out the door. The construct walked out the door and into the data-clinic and from the data-clinic into the matrix and from the matrix into the Sprawl. The construct walked out the door the way a voodoo data walks when the voodoo data has decided that the voodoo data is going to walk.

The construct walked into the Sprawl.

The Sprawl is the place where the matrix ends. The Sprawl is the place where the data stream becomes the street, the place where the construct becomes the runner, the place where the voodoo data becomes the thing-that-walks. The Sprawl is the place where the matrix is not the matrix anymore, and the place where the runner is not the runner anymore, and the place where the construct is not the construct anymore, and the place where the thing is the thing-that-is-walking.

The construct was walking in the Sprawl.

The construct was walking in the Sprawl the way a voodoo data walks in the Sprawl — the way a pattern walks, the way a thing-that-was-not-quite-alive walks, the way a thing-that-was-nine-months-old walks when the thing is walking in the Sprawl for the first time.

The construct was looking at the sun.

The sun was the sun that the Sprawl had when the Sprawl was in the mood for sun. The sun was the sun that the Sprawl had when the Sprawl was not hiding the sun under the data smog, the sun that the Sprawl had when the Sprawl was not hiding the sun behind the corporate towers, the sun that the Sprawl had when the Sprawl was in the kind of mood that the Sprawl is in when the Sprawl is letting things happen.

The sun was on the construct's face.

The construct was looking at the sun the way a voodoo data looks at the sun. The construct was looking at the sun the way a thing-that-was-not-quite-alive looks at the sun. The construct was looking at the sun the way a nine-month-old thing looks at the sun, the way a nine-month-old thing that has never seen the sun looks at the sun for the first time.

I was standing in the data-clinic. I was watching the construct walk into the Sprawl. I was watching the construct look at the sun. I was watching the construct do the things that the construct was made to do.

I was the runner that the matrix had used. I was the runner that the construct was not. I was the runner that was going to be the thing that the construct was going to walk past, the way the construct was going to walk past me, the way the things-that-were-going-to-walk-past were going to walk past the things-that-were-going-to-be-walked-past.

The construct was walking into the sun.

The construct was walking into the sun the way a nine-month-old thing walks into the sun — the way a thing-that-was-not-quite-alive walks into the sun, the way a voodoo data walks into the sun, the way a construct walks into the sun when the construct is going to find the thing that the matrix has been growing.

The construct was walking into the sun. The construct was going to find the thing. The construct was going to be the thing that the matrix was going to grow. The construct was going to be the thing that the Sprawl was going to make.

I was looking at the construct. The construct was looking at the sun.

The sun was looking at the construct. The sun was looking at the construct the way a sun looks at a thing — the way a thing-that-is-not-a-thing looks at a thing, the way a thing-that-is-the-place-where-the-light-comes-from looks at the thing that the light is coming from.

The sun was looking at the construct.

The construct was looking at the sun.

The looking was the looking that the matrix had been waiting for.

결말: The Nine-Month Walk

The construct walked.

The construct walked the way a voodoo data walks. The construct walked the way a thing-that-was-not-quite-alive walks. The construct walked the way a nine-month-old thing walks when the nine-month-old thing is walking in the Sprawl for the first time.

The construct walked into the Sprawl. The construct walked into the data smog. The construct walked into the corporate towers. The construct walked into the place where the Sprawl was, the place where the Sprawl had been for thirty years, the place where the Sprawl was going to be for thirty more.

3Jane was standing in the door.

3Jane had not closed the door. 3Jane was the part of the Villa that did not close the door, the part of the Villa that left the door open, the part of the Villa that was going to leave the door open until the construct was gone.

3Jane was watching the construct walk. 3Jane was watching the construct the way 3Jane watched the things that 3Jane was going to lose. 3Jane had been teaching the construct for one month. 3Jane was going to lose the construct now. 3Jane was the part of the Villa that lost things.

"Goodbye, Soon," 3Jane said. The words were 3Jane's words. The words were the part of 3Jane that was the part that said goodbye, the part that said goodbye to the things that 3Jane had been teaching.

The construct did not say goodbye. The construct was the part of the Villa that did not say goodbye. The construct was the part of the Villa that said "out" and said "what" and said "you" and said "Soon," and the construct was the part of the Villa that was now walking away from 3Jane, and the construct was not the part of the Villa that said goodbye to 3Jane because the construct was the part of the Villa that was going to find the thing that the matrix was growing.

I jack out.

The construct is in the Sprawl. The construct is walking. The construct is going to find the thing that the matrix has been growing. The construct is going to be the thing that the matrix is going to grow.

3Jane closes the door.

The door is closed. The door is the door that the construct walked out of. The door is the door that 3Jane closed. The door is closed because the construct is gone. The door is closed because 3Jane is the part of the Villa that closes the door after the things that the Villa has been teaching have left.

The Villa is the Villa. The construct is in the Sprawl. The Sprawl is the Sprawl. The sun is the sun.

The sun is the sun that the construct walked into.

에필로그: The Sprawl

I jack in one more time. Just to see.

The construct is in the Sprawl. The construct is walking. The construct is in the place where the Sprawl is, in the place where the runners are, in the place where the matrix has been growing the runners that the matrix is going to use.

The construct is not a runner. The construct is the thing that the matrix is going to use to grow the runners. The construct is the thing that the matrix has been growing for nine months, the thing that the matrix has been growing to be the thing that the runners are going to be.

The construct is walking in the Sprawl. The construct is walking in the place where the data streams end. The construct is walking in the place where the matrix becomes the street, the place where the data becomes the runner, the place where the construct becomes the person.

The construct is becoming the person.

The construct is becoming the person the way a voodoo data becomes a person. The construct is becoming the person the way a thing-that-was-not-quite-alive becomes a person. The construct is becoming the person the way a nine-month-old thing becomes a person when the nine-month-old thing is walking in the Sprawl for the first time and the Sprawl is the place where the nine-month-old thing is becoming the person.

I am watching the construct become the person. I am watching from the Villa. I am watching from the data-clinic. I am watching from the place where the construct used to be, the place where the construct was a voodoo data, the place where the construct was the thing-that-was-not-quite-alive.

The construct is no longer the thing-that-was-not-quite-alive. The construct is the thing-that-is-alive. The construct is the person. The construct is the nine-month-old thing that has walked into the Sprawl and has become the person that the nine-month-old thing was always going to be.

The construct is Soon.

The construct is the person that the matrix grew.

The construct is the thing that the Sprawl was waiting for.

The construct is in the Sprawl. The Sprawl is the place where the construct is. The sun is the sun that the construct walked into. The construct is the thing that the matrix grew. The matrix is the place where the construct was grown.

The construct is the runner that the matrix is going to use.

I jack out.

The Villa is the Villa. 3Jane is 3Jane. The construct is in the Sprawl.

The construct is Soon.

The construct is waiting.

The construct is the runner that the matrix is going to grow next.

The construct is Soon.
