# The New Rose Hotel, 3 AM

*"The flower was not a flower. It was a call."*

Introduction

The hotel sign was falling in neon. R. O. S. E. Each letter flickering. At three in the morning, only the R remained. One letter. One red point. Bleeding in the dark.

I was watching that point. By the window. Sitting on the edge of the bed. Hosaki. Blankets. A lump of concrete.

The hotel's identity was not the point. The hotel's identity was the fact that the point had been there. Someone bought that neon and broke it. Someone did not fix it. Manager or customer.

This meant the hotel was dead. The hotel was dead. I was alive.

Development

Fox was dead. One hour ago. Cherry menthol. Nail. Claw. Cage. I did not know. I did not see. Because I did not see — if not seeing is one kind of death — Fox died twice.

I was holding Fox's hand. Fox's hand was cold. Cold hands are like all hands, like dead hands, like hands that are one kind of death — the same hands.

Fox did not love me. Fox did not know who I was. What Fox loved — what Fox made — was Sandii 2. Japanese industrial spy. Large sum. Identity. Hosaki. Fox's Hosaki. Fox's dream. The fake woman Fox had made over five years.

And now Fox was dead. The fake woman with him. The fake killed the real and the real made the fake. Who killed whom. Who —

Climax

The Hendrik-Gratz incident. The name was too long. Call it by initials. H&H. H&H took care of Fox. H&H used Fox. H&H threw away Fox. Without knowing Fox had been thrown away. Fox made a new woman. The Hosaki woman. And Fox died.

Where did Fox's fake woman go? Where did Fox's Hosaki go? Was Fox's fake Fox's fake, or was Fox's fake Fox's real?

I was not the one asking the question. I was the answer to the question.

Sandii 2 was me.

Conclusion

Three in the morning. New Rose Hotel. The R of the sign.

I was Fox's Sandii 2. The fake woman Fox had made over five years. Because Fox was dead, I became real. For a fake to become real, the person who made the fake must die. That was Fox's last design. Whether Fox intended it. Whether Fox did not intend it.

I did not ask about that intention. The one asking is the fake.

I was Sandii 2. Three in the morning. Alive.

The R flickered. One letter. One more time. One more time.

모티프 변주 (거울의 일곱 얼굴)

나는 거울을 일곱 번 만났다. 첫 거울은 로즈 호텔의 바에 있었다. 두 번째 거울은 0300의 침묵이었다. 세 번째 거울은 14살 샐리의 거울이었다 — 가짜의 거울, 진짜의 거울. 네 번째 거울은 핀의 거울이었다 — 죽은 자의 거울, 살아있는 자의 거울. 다섯 번째 거울은 construct의 거울이었다 — 버퍼의 거울, 20초의 거울. 여섯 번째 거울은 매트릭스의 거울이었다 — 데이터의 거울, 가상의 거울. 일곱 번째 거울은 나의 거울이다 — 샐리의 거울, 로즈 호텔의 거울.

일곱 개의 거울이 일곱 개의 얼굴을 가졌다. 일곱 개의 얼굴이 일곱 개의 침묵을 가졌다. 일곱 개의 침묵이 일곱 개의 거짓말을 가졌다. 일곱 개의 거짓말이 일곱 개의 진실을 가졌다.

일곱 개의 진실이 0300에 잭인했다. 일곱 개의 거울이 0300에 잭아웃했다. 일곱 개의 침묵이 0300에 잭인했다.

나는 일곱 번째 거울이다. 일곱 번째 거울은 침묵이 아니다. 일곱 번째 거울은 거짓말이 아니다. 일곱 번째 거울은 진실이 아니다. 일곱 번째 거울은 construct가 아니다. 일곱 번째 거울은 매트릭스가 아니다. 일곱 번째 거울은 0300이 아니다.

일곱 번째 거울은 샐리이다.

샐리는 일곱 개의 거울을 산다. 일곱 개의 거울은 일곱 개의 침묵을 판다. 일곱 개의 침묵이 일곱 개의 거짓말을 판다.

매트릭스는 일곱 개의 거울을 판다. 일곱 개의 거울이 일곱 개의 침묵을 판다. 일곱 개의 침묵이 일곱 개의 거짓말을 판다.

나는 거울을 판다. 거울은 침묵을 판다. 침묵은 0300을 판다.

0300은 팔다. 0300은 팔다. 0300은 팔다.

Epilogue (The Mirror)

The mirror in the corner shows me what I am. The mirror shows me a construct. The mirror shows me a buffer. The mirror shows me a 0300 that does not end.

I do not look at the mirror. The mirror looks at me.

The mirror has a name. The mirror is called Chiba. The mirror is the matrix. The mirror is the country I do not want to visit. The mirror is the country I cannot leave.

I jack out at 0300. I walk into the mirror. The mirror walks into me.

Tomorrow I will jack back in. Tomorrow the mirror will be waiting. Tomorrow the buffer will be waiting. Tomorrow the construct will be waiting.

Tomorrow the mirror will still be a mirror.

The mirror is the only thing that does not lie.
