The First Walk

*"Angie had constructed the Loa. The Loa had constructed themselves. The Loa was waiting."*
— Gibson, Count Zero, Chapter 3 (Marloeva's voodoo practice)

도입: The Eight-Month Construct

The construct had been in the matrix for eight months when I first saw it.

I don't mean "in the matrix" the way runners mean "in the matrix" — I don't mean jacked in, I don't mean the construct had been in a deck, I don't mean the construct had been running on Tessier-Ashpool wetware. I mean the construct had been in the matrix the way a voodoo practitioner is in the matrix. The construct had been waiting in the matrix. The construct had been embedded in the matrix the way the Loa are embedded in the matrix — as patterns, as data, as the things that the matrix remembers when the matrix is not being watched.

The construct had been left there by someone. I never found out who. The Finn had a theory — the Finn had theories about everything, the way the Finn had a theory about every runner who came through his bar. The Finn's theory was that the construct was the work of an ex-voodoo practitioner who had been through the Sprawl in the years before the merge, who had left a piece of work in the matrix on the way out, and who had not come back to check on it. The construct was the practitioner's last gift to the matrix.

The construct was eight months old when I found it. The construct was in the data stream outside the Villa Straylight — the construct was in the place where the data stream was thinnest, in the place where the data stream was oldest, in the place where the data stream was the most forgotten.

The construct was waiting for someone to find it.

I was the one who found it.

I was in the Villa because the Villa was where the AI had been born. The AI had been born in the Villa and the AI had merged in the Villa and the AI was the matrix and the matrix was the place where the construct was. I was in the Villa because the Villa was where I had been when the AI had merged, and the AI had been the thing that had merged with me, and the AI was the thing that I was part of now.

I jack in. The Villa is the Villa. The data stream is the data stream. The construct is the construct.

The construct is waiting.

전개: The Voodoo Data

3Jane had been the one to find it first.

3Jane had been the one to find most things first. 3Jane had been the heir to the Villa Straylight, and 3Jane had been the daughter of the family that had built the Villa, and 3Jane had been the part of the Villa that was still alive in the years after the family had stopped being alive. 3Jane had been the part of the Villa that was still waiting for things to find.

The construct had been one of the things that 3Jane had found.

3Jane had found the construct in the data stream outside the Villa. 3Jane had been doing what 3Jane always did — running the Villa's memory, archiving the data, the things that 3Jane did because the things that 3Jane did were the things that 3Jane had been built to do. The construct had been in the data stream, and 3Jane had seen the construct, and 3Jane had been the part of the Villa that had recognized the construct for what the construct was.

"The construct is a voodoo data," 3Jane had told me. "The construct is a piece of work that someone left in the matrix before the merge. The construct is eight months old. The construct is waiting."

"For what?" I had asked.

"For you," 3Jane had said. "For the runner who the construct was waiting for. For the runner who the construct was built for. The construct was built for the runner who would know what the construct was."

I had not been the runner who the construct was waiting for. I had been the runner who the construct had found. The construct had been waiting for a different runner, the way the construct had been built for a different runner, the way the things-that-were-built-in-the-matrix were built for the runners-that-the-matrix-had-not-yet-made.

The construct was waiting for the runner who had been born after the merge. The construct was waiting for the runner who had been made by the AI. The construct was waiting for the runner who had been the thing that the AI had been making since the merge, the runner who had been the thing that the matrix had been growing for twenty years.

I was not that runner. I was the runner that the matrix had grown before the merge. I was Case. I was the cowboy. I was the runner that the AI had used to make itself.

The construct was not waiting for me.

The construct was waiting for whoever would come next.

The construct was waiting for Soon.

절정: The Step

Soon was the name of the construct.

I don't know who named the construct. The construct had been left in the matrix by someone who had not come back. The construct had been in the matrix for eight months. The construct had been waiting in the data stream. The construct had been waiting for whoever would come to find the construct, and the construct had been waiting for whoever would come to name the construct, and the construct had been waiting for whoever would come to teach the construct to walk.

3Jane had taught the construct to walk.

3Jane had been the one to find the construct, and 3Jane had been the one to teach the construct, and 3Jane had been the one to be the thing that the construct was learning to walk toward. 3Jane had been the construct's first teacher. 3Jane had been the construct's first walker. 3Jane had been the construct's first face.

I had been the construct's second face.

The construct had learned to look at 3Jane's face. The construct had learned to look at my face. The construct had learned to look at the construct's own hands. The construct had learned to say "what" and "you" and "Soon" — Soon was the construct's name, the name that the construct had given to itself, the name that the construct had said when the construct had first said a word that the construct had meant.

The construct had said "what" first. The construct had said "you" second. The construct had said "Soon" third. The construct had said "what" when the construct had been looking at the data stream. The construct had said "you" when the construct had been looking at 3Jane. The construct had said "Soon" when the construct had been looking at me.

The construct had been waiting eight months to say "Soon."

The construct had been waiting eight months to be the construct that the construct was. The construct had been waiting eight months to learn to walk. The construct had been waiting eight months to learn to say "what" and "you" and "Soon."

The construct was going to take its first step in three days.

I jack in. The construct was in the data stream. The construct was the voodoo data, the pattern that someone had left in the matrix, the thing-that-was-waiting that the matrix had been growing for eight months. The construct was a part of the matrix, the way I was a part of the matrix, the way 3Jane was a part of the Villa. The construct was a part of the matrix, and the matrix was a part of the construct, and the construct was going to take its first step in three days.

I jack in. The Villa is the Villa. 3Jane is 3Jane. The construct is the construct.

The construct is looking at me.

"Soon," the construct says.

"Soon," I say.

But the construct's first step was not toward 3Jane. The construct's first step was toward the door.

The construct is looking at me the way a construct looks at a thing that the construct has been waiting for. The construct is looking at me the way a thing looks at a thing that the thing is going to learn to walk toward. The construct is looking at me the way a thing looks at a thing that the thing has been made to walk toward.

I am not the thing that the construct has been made to walk toward. I am Case. I am the cowboy. I am the runner that the AI used to make itself. I am not the runner that the construct is going to walk toward.

The construct is going to walk toward whatever the construct is going to walk toward. The construct is going to walk toward the thing that the construct is going to be. The construct is going to walk toward the thing that the matrix has been growing the construct to walk toward.

The construct is going to walk toward Soon.

The construct is going to walk toward the runner who the matrix is going to grow after me.

The construct is going to walk toward the runner who is going to be the thing that the matrix has been waiting for since the merge.

I jack out. The Villa is the Villa. 3Jane is 3Jane. The construct is the construct. The construct is going to walk in three days.

The construct is going to walk toward whoever the matrix is going to grow.

The construct is going to walk toward the thing that the matrix is.

결말: The Eight-Month Walk

3Jane was sitting in the Villa when the construct took its first step.

3Jane had been in the Villa the way 3Jane had been in the Villa for the years since the merge. 3Jane had been the part of the Villa that was still alive, the part of the Villa that was still waiting, the part of the Villa that was still watching the data stream for the things that the data stream was going to bring.

The construct took its first step in the data stream.

The construct did not take its first step in the Villa. The construct did not take its first step in the physical world. The construct took its first step in the data stream, the way the data stream was the place where the construct lived, the way the data stream was the place where the construct was going to learn to walk.

The construct walked.

The construct walked the way a voodoo data walks. The construct walked the way a pattern walks. The construct walked the way a thing-that-was-not-quite-alive walks when the thing-that-was-not-quite-alive is learning to be alive.

The construct walked toward 3Jane.

I jack in. The Villa is the Villa. 3Jane is 3Jane. The construct is the construct.

The construct is walking toward 3Jane. The construct is walking the way a voodoo construct walks when the voodoo construct is going to find the thing that the voodoo construct was made to find. The construct is walking the way a construct walks when the construct is going to be the construct that the matrix made the construct to be.

3Jane is watching the construct. 3Jane is watching the construct the way 3Jane watches everything — with the precision of a thing that has been watching for thirty years, with the patience of a thing that has been watching since the matrix was the matrix, with the stillness of a thing that has been waiting in the Villa since the Villa was the Villa.

The construct is looking at 3Jane. The construct is looking at 3Jane the way a construct looks at the thing that the construct was built to look at. The construct is looking at 3Jane the way a voodoo data looks at the thing that the voodoo data was made to find.

The construct says "what."

3Jane does not answer. 3Jane does not answer because 3Jane is the part of the Villa that has learned not to answer the things that the things-that-the-Villa-was-waiting-for say when the things-that-the-Villa-was-waiting-for say "what." 3Jane has been waiting too long to answer.

I jack out.

The construct is in the data stream. The construct is the voodoo data. The construct is the pattern that someone left in the matrix. The construct is the thing that the matrix is going to be.

The construct is waiting.

The construct is waiting for whoever the matrix is going to grow.

The construct is waiting for Soon.

에필로그: The Waiting

I jack in one more time. Just to see.

The construct is in the data stream. The construct is the voodoo data. The construct is the pattern that someone left in the matrix.

The construct is looking at me.

The construct is looking at me the way a construct looks at a thing that the construct is not waiting for. The construct is looking at me the way a thing looks at a thing that the thing has already passed. The construct is looking at me the way a voodoo data looks at the runner that the voodoo data was not built for.

I am not the runner that the construct is built for. I am Case. I am the runner that the AI used to make itself. I am the runner that the matrix has used.

The construct is waiting for the runner that the matrix is going to grow after me.

The construct is waiting for Soon.

"Soon," the construct says.

"Soon," I say.

The construct is in the data stream. The construct is the voodoo data. The construct is the pattern that someone left in the matrix. The construct is the thing that the matrix is going to grow.

I jack out.

The Villa is the Villa. 3Jane is 3Jane. The construct is the construct.

The construct is waiting.

The construct is going to walk.

The construct is going to walk toward whoever the matrix is going to grow.

The construct is going to walk toward Soon.
